🏫 About San Pedro Elementary School

San Pedro Elementary School (School ID #108941), established in 1978, is a public elementary school located at Sitio Centro, Brgy San Pedro, Mulanay, Quezon under Mulanay District II, Division of Quezon, Region IV-A CALABARZON. We provide quality K-12 basic education to 97 learners through a holistic, learner-centered approach.

🎯 Vision

We dream of Filipinos who passionately love their country and whose values and competencies enable them to realize their full potential and contribute meaningfully to building the nation.

🚀 Mission

To protect and promote the right of every Filipino to quality, equitable, culture-based, and complete basic education in a child-friendly, gender-sensitive, safe, and motivating environment.

⭐ Core Values

Maka-Diyos • Makatao • Makakalikasan • Makabansa — nurturing holistic development in every learner of San Pedro.

📘 Programs Offered

Kindergarten, Grades 1–6 (Mother Tongue, Filipino, English, Math, Science, Araling Panlipunan, EPP/TLE, MAPEH, ESP)
