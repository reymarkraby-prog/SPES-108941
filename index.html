Who We Are

A Place Where
Learning Comes Alive

San Pedro Elementary School (#108941) is dedicated to providing a safe, inclusive, and stimulating environment where students from kindergarten through 6th grade can discover their potential. Our passionate educators combine time-tested teaching practices with innovative approaches to inspire a genuine love of learning.

  • Academic excellence through personalized instruction
  • Character education and social-emotional learning
  • Inclusive community welcoming all families
  • Enrichment programs in arts, STEM, and physical education
  • Strong parent and community partnerships
🌟

Our Mission

To inspire every child to reach their highest potential through rigorous, joyful, and equitable education.
